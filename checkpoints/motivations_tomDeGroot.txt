The story of "Tom de Groot" is motivated by Tom's lively imagination and his passion for drawing comic strips. He is motivated to express himself creatively through his art rather than conforming to traditional expectations like doing homework. Tom's constant strive to outsmart his brother, Derek, and his teacher, meneer Fullerman, also drives the plot forward. Additionally, Tom's strong bond with his dog serves as a source of motivation and companionship for him throughout the story. Ultimately, the book highlights the importance of staying true to oneself, pursuing one's passions, and finding joy in everyday life.
