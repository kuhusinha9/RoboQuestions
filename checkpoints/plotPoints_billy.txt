The key plot events in the text are:

1. Billy discovers a secret passage to a world of giants.
2. Billy befriends the gentle giant Bumble who teaches him about the ways of the giants.
3. Billy and Bumble decide to defeat the evil giant Gorgon who is causing turmoil in the giant world.
4. They embark on a dangerous journey filled with challenges and obstacles.
5. Along the way, they meet various characters such as the wise old giant Sage and the cheerful giantess Joy.
6. In a thrilling showdown with Gorgon, Billy uses the lessons he learned from Bumble and the other giants to defeat him and free the giant world.
7. Billy returns to his village as a hero, with new friends and unforgettable memories of his giant adventure.
