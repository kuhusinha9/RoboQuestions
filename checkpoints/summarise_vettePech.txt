Bram had a terrible summer vacation and is looking forward to forgetting it with the start of a new school year. The year starts well when he passes the "Cheese Touch" to Jeremy, but soon disaster after disaster occurs. The worst moment is when Bram accidentally hides in the girls' bathroom, a humiliation he wants to keep secret but which quickly spreads around the school. The new school year is not going as well as Bram had hoped, and he struggles to survive it.
