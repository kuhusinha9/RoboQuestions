Motivations in "Het meisje dat de maan dronk":

- Luna is motivated by her desire to control and harness her magical moon powers. She wants to understand herself and her abilities better, to be able to use them for good.
- Xan is motivated by her love and compassion for Luna and the abandoned babies she saves from the villagers' sacrifices. She wants to protect and nurture Luna, guiding her to use her powers wisely.
- The villagers are motivated by their fear of the unknown and their belief in old traditions. They sacrifice babies to the witch out of fear, seeking to appease an imagined threat.
- The story is driven by themes of love, courage, acceptance, and the power of understanding and empathy in overcoming prejudices and fears.
