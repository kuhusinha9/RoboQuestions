**What motivates the story:**
- The story is motivated by the theme of friendship and acceptance, as Julia forms a unique bond with Zara the zebra and helps her navigate her feelings of being different.
- The desire for adventure and connection also motivates the story, as Julia is always seeking new experiences and forms a special connection with Zara because they both feel a sense of being different from others.

**What motivates the characters:**
- Julia is motivated by her curiosity and sense of empathy towards Zara, wanting to help her feel accepted and confident in her own uniqueness.
- Zara is motivated by a desire to fit in and feel like she belongs, ultimately finding the courage to embrace her differences with Julia's support.
- Both characters are motivated by the values of friendship, self-acceptance, and celebrating individuality, which drive their actions and decisions throughout the story.
