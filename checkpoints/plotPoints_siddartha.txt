The key plot events in "Siddhartha" include:

1. Siddhartha leaves home to seek spiritual enlightenment and joins the Samanas.
2. Siddhartha realizes that the Samanas cannot provide him with the answers he seeks.
3. Siddhartha and Govinda encounter the teachings of the Buddha but Siddhartha decides to find his own path to enlightenment.
4. Siddhartha becomes a wealthy merchant and falls in love with Kamala, a courtesan.
5. Despite his material success, Siddhartha feels unfulfilled.
6. Siddhartha eventually finds peace as a simple ferryman by the river.
7. Siddhartha meets his old friend Govinda and shares his insights on life and the pursuit of enlightenment.
