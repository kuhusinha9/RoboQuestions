The key plot events in the text "Warrior Cats" by Erin Hunter include:

1. Rusty, a house cat, decides to join ThunderClan and becomes Firepaw, beginning his training to become a warrior.
2. Firepaw discovers Tigerclaw's plot to betray Bluestar and take over the clan.
3. Firepaw, now Fireheart, must prove his loyalty and struggles with the challenges of living in the wild.
4. Fireheart is initiated as a full-fledged warrior and reveals Tigerclaw's betrayal.
5. Fireheart becomes the new leader of ThunderClan, Firestar, and restores peace between the clans.

These events drive the story forward, exploring themes of courage, friendship, betrayal, and survival in the world of wild cats.
