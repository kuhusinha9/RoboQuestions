Here are the key plot events in "Dog Man":

1. The police officer and his dog are seriously injured in an accident.
2. The surgeon decides to merge them together, creating Dog Man.
3. Dog Man becomes a unique hero with the senses of a dog and the body of a man.
4. Dog Man must save the city from the evil cat Petey, who is always plotting to take over the world.
5. Despite not being the smartest, Dog Man is a loyal and courageous friend.
6. The story includes humorous moments of Dog Man trying to adjust to his new life and Petey's failed schemes.
7. The book teaches important lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the battle between good and evil.
